Upload Issuer Information

File Format Requirements

Please ensure your CSV or Excel file follows this format:

  • issuer_name - Company name of the issuer (required)
  • contact_name_1 - Name of first contact
  • contact_email_1 - Email of first contact
  • contact_name_2 - Name of second contact (optional)
  • contact_email_2 - Email of second contact (optional)
  • ... and so on up to contact_name_5 and contact_email_5

Example row: "Acme Corp", "John Doe", "john@acme.com", "Jane Smith", "jane@acme.com", ...
